Total emissions, in the simplest form, are determined by:
  • Population: number of people
  • Per capita impact: average emissions per person
Gross Domestic Product GDP is the monetary value of all goods and services produced within a country or region in a specific time period.
Is there a relationship between economic growth and carbon dioxide emissions?
When we look at countries' GDP and the CO2 emissions, it seems like there is a positive correlation between the GDP and the CO2 emissions. However, in some countries, like United Kingdom and Germany, there is a slight change of CO2 emissions in recent years.
